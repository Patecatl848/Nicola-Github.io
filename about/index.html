About

Hello, my name is Nicola and this is my personal website. Here I keep memories and thoughts. Every once in a while I even post some technical stuff.

I’m a coder at heart author of several open source projects, a Microsoft MVP, Conference Speaker, teacher and consultant. I live in Ravenna, Italy, where I work on the Amica 10 project. In my hometown I run the local CoderDojo, a coding club for kids. I am also the creator and organizer of DevRomagna, the local developer community.

I am a former runner, climber and swimmer now keeping myself busy with bodyweight training. I love reading good books, hiking in the wilderness and riding my Triumph Bonneville. My all time favourite quote:

Only those who will risk going too far can possibly find out how far one can go – T.S. Eliot
